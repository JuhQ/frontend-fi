Speakers

  • Mathias Bynens (@mathias)

    Speaker topic: JavaScript and Unicode

    Mathias is a Belgian web standards freak. He likes HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Unicode, performance, and security. At Opera Software he’s a member of the Developer Relations team.

  • Andrea Giammarchi (@webreflection)

    Speaker topic: Custom Elements / Web Components

    Web, Mobile, IoT, and all JS things since 00's. Formerly JS engineer at @nokia, @facebook, @twitter.

  • Harry Roberts (@csswizardry)

    Speaker topic: 4½ Methods for Theming in (S)CSS

    Harry is an award-winning Consultant Front-end Architect, designer, developer, writer, and speaker from the UK. Previously a Senior Developer at BSkyB, he now helps teams all over the world build better products.

    He specialises in authoring and scaling product-scale UIs; he writes on the subjects of maintainability, architecture, performance, and more at csswizardry.com; he is the lead developer of inuitcss, a powerful, scalable, Sass-based, BEM, OOCSS framework; he Tweets at @csswizardry.
